AMPHIBIANS

Frogs, Salamanders, Caecillians, and more!

Amphibians are a massive group, spanning over 7000+ species and over at least three subclasses. Traditionally, Amphibians are defined as cold-blooded vertebrates who have two life stages. The word originates from Greek roots, roughly translating to “two” (Greek amphi) “life” (Greek bios) stages ^1.
